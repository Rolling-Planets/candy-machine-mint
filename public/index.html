Roadmap

Inception

The creation of the project. Buiding all the assets, code, and minting page. Fine-tuning algorithms and setting up everything for launch.

Community Building

Creating and building the community from the ground-up. We are creating and setting-up the Discord Channel, Twitter, and Instagram page to get exposure and start marketing the project.

Minting phase

This is our launch. Everyone can mint our animated NFts and get their animated as well as static NFTs to use as profile picture and resell on the Secondary Market.

Extra NFTs distribution

Each one of our Holders will recieve an extra NFT. There may or may not be animated,. However, they will also each have unique metadata and rarity.

This phase is all about community. We will build a governance system so people can vode the fututre of the project and community. It’s the nature of decentralization to give power tot he community.

Live Minting

Phase 2 is extremely exciting. First of all, the NFTs of this phase will be in 3D, and holders will actually be able to navigate on their planet. What is extremely important is that they will be minted regularily, and each NFT will be unique and based on a newly discovered planet by the most advanced Telescope built by humanity (James Webb Telescope).
